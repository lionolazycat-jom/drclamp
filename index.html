Product Overview

What Is the
Dr. B™ Clamp?

The Dr. B™ Clamp is a sterile, single-use circumcision device developed by Dr. Hasbi, a specialist circumcision surgeon with extensive operative experience. Engineered as a two-part assembly, it fits securely over the glans to enable a controlled, haemostatic excision of the prepuce.

Unlike conventional methods, the Dr. B™ Clamp reduces operative variability by standardising tissue compression and excision angle — delivering a reproducible, aesthetically consistent outcome across all patient demographics.

The Device

Three engineered features.

Each feature addresses a specific clinical problem — control, comfort, and cosmetic outcome.

Feature 01 — Compression

3-Teeth Differential Clips

Creates differential compression for better control and a more precise, even result compared to a uniform clamp.

Feature 02 — Removal

Extended Longer Tube

Allows gentle clamping and low-discomfort removal — straightforward for clinic staff and for parents at home.

Feature 03 — Aesthetics

45° Anatomical Slant

Follows the natural shape of the glans for a consistent cosmetic result — the design detail that makes Dr. B Clamp™ visually distinct.

How It Works

Four steps. ~15 minutes.

Step 01

Anaesthetise

Local anaesthetic administered. Lasts up to 2–3 hours, with pain relief for the following hours.

Step 02

Size & Position

Correct size selected from the 13–30mm range and positioned over the foreskin.

Step 03

Clamp & Cut

Differential compression secures the tissue. No sutures. ~15–20 minutes.*

Step 04
